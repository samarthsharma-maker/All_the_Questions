## Additional Notes for Question Creators

### Story Development Guidelines (If Scenario-Based = Yes)
1. **Make it realistic:** Base on actual industry problems
2. **Add business context:** Include cost/impact numbers
3. **Create urgency:** Why this needs to be fixed now
4. **Define stakeholders:** Who cares about this being fixed
5. **Add personality:** Give the company/people character

### Technical Difficulty Calibration

**Easy Level:**
- Single technology focus
- 3-5 clear steps
- Limited troubleshooting required
- 20-30 minutes completion time

**Medium Level:**
- Single technology with complexity
- 5-8 steps with some complexity
- Moderate troubleshooting needed
- 30-45 minutes completion time

**Hard Level:**
- Single technology with high complexity
- 8+ steps with complexity
- Significant troubleshooting required
- 45-90 minutes completion time

### Assignment vs Contest Guidelines

**Use Assignment Format When:**
- Teaching specific workflows
- Building foundational skills
- Step-by-step learning is beneficial
- Multiple deliverables required
- Documentation is important

**Use Contest Format When:**
- Testing problem-solving under constraints
- Simulating production incidents
- Single focused objective
- Speed matters
- Time pressure is a factor
